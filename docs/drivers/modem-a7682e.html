drv_modem_a7682e

Cellular modem driver for the SIMCOM A7682E (4G LTE Cat-1). C driver wrapping the Espressif esp_modem component.

Overview Proven

HAL TypeModem
ChipA7682E
InterfaceUART
DescriptionHandles AT commands, PPP data mode for IP connectivity, and SMS send/receive. One of two non-Rust (C) drivers. SIM card required.

LilyGo T-Display-S3

Compact ESP32-S3 board with a small 320x170 LCD and capacitive touch. Touch-only input.

Overview

MCUESP32-S3 (dual-core Xtensa LX7, 240 MHz)
Flash16 MB
PSRAM8 MB
ConnectivityWiFi 4 + BLE 5.0 (on-chip)
StatusSupported

Components

ComponentChipInterfaceDriverStatus
LCD displayST7789 320×170SPIdrv_lcd_st7789Proven
TouchCST816I2Cdrv_touch_cst816Fixed

Board config

Config file: tdisplay-s3.json

Located at sdcard_layout/config/boards/tdisplay-s3.json in the source tree.

Notes

  • Touch driver had cold-boot, auto-sleep, and gesture issues that have been fixed.
  • No physical keyboard — touch-only input.
  • 2 hardware buttons available on GPIO for additional input.
